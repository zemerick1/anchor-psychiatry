Our Mission

The values and principles that guide everything we do.

At Anchor Psychiatry, our mission is to provide exceptional, patient-centered psychiatric care that empowers individuals to achieve lasting mental wellness through evidence-based treatment, compassionate support, and personalized medication management.

Our Purpose

We founded Anchor Psychiatry with a simple belief: that quality mental healthcare should be accessible, compassionate, and grounded in the latest clinical evidence. Our team of board-certified psychiatric nurse practitioners is dedicated to serving the Cookeville community and surrounding areas with the highest standard of psychiatric care.

We understand that seeking help for mental health concerns takes courage. That's why we've built a practice centered on trust, respect, and genuine partnership with every patient who walks through our doors.

Our Approach

We take a whole-person approach to psychiatric care, recognizing that mental health is deeply connected to every aspect of a person's life. Rather than offering one-size-fits-all solutions, we take the time to listen, understand your unique circumstances, and develop a treatment plan tailored specifically to your needs.

Our evidence-based approach combines thorough psychiatric evaluations with individualized medication management and psychotherapy. We believe in starting conservatively, monitoring closely, and adjusting thoughtfully — because your safety and well-being are always our top priority.

Our Values

Compassion: We treat every patient with empathy, dignity, and respect. We create a safe, judgment-free environment where you can speak openly about your challenges and goals.

Excellence: We are committed to staying at the forefront of psychiatric medicine, continuously advancing our knowledge and skills to provide the best possible care.

Collaboration: We work closely with your existing healthcare providers — primary care physicians, therapists, and specialists — to ensure coordinated, holistic treatment that addresses your complete health.

Accessibility: We believe that geography and circumstance should never be barriers to quality care. Through both in-person and telehealth appointments, we strive to make specialized psychiatric services available to all who need them.

Our Commitment to the Community

As members of the Cookeville community, we are invested in the well-being of our neighbors. We are committed to reducing the stigma surrounding mental health, advocating for greater access to psychiatric services, and fostering a community where seeking help is seen as a sign of strength.

Whether you are navigating a new diagnosis, managing a long-term condition, or simply looking for support during a difficult time — Anchor Psychiatry is here to be your steady foundation.
