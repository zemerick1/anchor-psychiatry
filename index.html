Our Services

We offer a focused range of psychiatric services designed to deliver lasting, meaningful outcomes.

Comprehensive Psychiatric Evaluations

Thorough initial assessments reviewing symptoms, medical and mental health history, and life circumstances to establish an accurate diagnosis and an individualized treatment plan.

Medication Management

Ongoing clinical oversight of psychiatric medications — including careful initiation, dosage optimization, side-effect monitoring, and education — ensuring safe, effective treatment.

Telehealth Appointments

Convenient, secure virtual visits that bring specialized psychiatric care to the comfort of your own home — with the same quality and confidentiality as in-person sessions.

Spravato® (Esketamine) Treatment

FDA-approved nasal spray therapy for treatment-resistant depression, administered in our certified clinic under medical supervision. Spravato® offers a breakthrough option for patients who haven't responded to traditional antidepressants.
